Museum Rental

The Kentucky Derby Museum provides a unique Louisville setting for your next event! With our versatile indoor and outdoor space, and flexible rental plans, our exciting venue can be customized for your individual taste and style. From the classic elegance of The Great Hall, exhibit and gallery spaces to the scenic Kentucky Oaks Terrace, options exude the thrills of a day at the races!

View Rental Spaces

Amenities may include:

  • Unique indoor and outdoor spaces accommodating up to 1000 people
  • On-site Event Host with personalized service
  • One stop source for all your rental needs including catering, décor, AV, set-up, clean-up dance-floor, bugler, bourbon tastings and more
  • Private exhibition experience
  • Guided Tours - Let our experienced and knowledgeable guides give your guests a private tour of Historic Churchill Downs Racetrack
  • Custom Gift Selection - The Museum Gift Shop will be happy to create a custom gift package for your guests! Call us at 1-800-KY-DERBY (800-593-3729), click here and we'll put together a custom package for you.

The Great Hall

The Great Hall, a one-of-a-kind area allows for an open environment seating up to 270 guests banquet style or up to 400 guests reception style.

Share your story on 360° movie screens or immerse your guests into the first Saturday in May with The Greatest Race, our award winning signature exhibit movie.

Larger groups can be served through multi-level use of the Museum with up to 1500 people accommodated for cocktail receptions.

The Pollard Gallery

The Carl F. Pollard Gallery is spacious area on the second floor with a series of windows across the front of the room and a balcony view overlooking Gate 1 that can accommodate 130 guests seated at round tables of 10, 150 guests seated in theater style, or 90 guests seated in classroom style.

The space is equipped with all necessary audio and visual for meeting presentations.

The Derby Café

Perfect for intimate dining, the space overlooks the outdoor Oaks Garden Terrace and stable area with dinner seating up to 110 people. This space is perfect for a breakfast gathering, bridal shower or intimate dinner meeting.

The Oaks Garden Terrace

The outdoor Oaks Garden Terrace is perfect for our ceremony, special event or reception under the stars! The space captures the essence of Derby and is home to our miniature companion horse Winston and a Resident Thoroughbred…who doesn’t want to invite a horse to their party?
