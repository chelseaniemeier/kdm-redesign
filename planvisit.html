General Admission

Hourly 30-minute historic walking tour of Churchill Downs Racetrack • “The Greatest Race,” a 360° 18-minute media experience that will make your heart race and emotions soar • Two floors of family-friendly interactive exhibits including our resident Thoroughbred and Miniature horse • Access to our Gift Shop & Kentucky fare in the Derby Café • And much more!

Additional discounts provided by the Kentucky Derby Museum: Active Military, Track employee (Racing ID required), Pre-scheduled groups of 15 people or more together, and organized school groups. The Museum reserves the right to alter discounts and prices at any time.
Admission Pricing
Adults (15-64) $15
Seniors (65+) $14
Children (5-14) $8
Children (Under 5) Free
All times shown are Eastern Time. The Museum is closed Kentucky Oaks and Kentucky Derby Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve and Christmas Day. We recommend you arrive at least 90 minutes before we close to enjoy the full experience. The last Historic Walking Tour of the day begins 30 minutes before we close.
Museum Hours
Monday - Saturday 8 a.m. - 5 p.m.
Sunday 11 a.m. - 5 p.m.

Specialty Tours

The Kentucky Derby Museum offers several specialty tours to enhance your General Admission experience:

Barn and Backside Van Tour

Your opportunity to experience areas of the Backside of Churchill Downs Racetrack and see Thoroughbreds in their environment that are closed off to the general public and only offered to Museum guests!

$15 per person (plus Museum Admission required)

Behind the Scenes Walking Tour

This tour gives special access to exclusive areas of Churchill Downs Racetrack; truly an insider’s view of the non-public areas including the Jockey's Quarters, Millionaires Row, private member’s-only Turf Club, and the other newly renovated spaces - bring your camera for the picturesque views you will spot on this tour like the fourth floor balcony, legendary Twin Spires, track, and the city.

$15 per person (plus Museum Admission required); Children under 5 FREE

Around the Track Golf Cart Tour

Join us for the Superfecta of tours and embark on the most exclusive tour Kentucky Derby Museum has to offer! The Around the Track Golf Cart Tour takes you through the history, pageantry and legacy of the Churchill Downs Racetrack and the Kentucky Derby.

$99 per person (includes lunch at a local restaurant and General Admission to the Museum)

Museum Accessibility

  • The Museum and the Historic Walking Tour are wheelchair accessible.
  • Free wheelchair rentals are available on a first-come, first-served basis upon request. You are welcome to bring your own manual wheelchair, motorized wheelchair or electric mobility scooter.
  • An induction loop is available for hearing impaired.
  • If requested at least 10 days prior to your visit, the Museum will provide a sign language interpreter.
