Volunteer at the Kentucky Derby Museum with The Outriders Society

The Outriders Society is the official volunteer organization of the Kentucky Derby Museum. The purpose of The Outriders is to spread our mission, enhance the image of the Museum throughout the community and assist the team. The Outriders offer support by participating in research and cataloging, preparing mailings, greeting guests, helping staff with special events and administrative responsibilities and so much more. We greatly value our volunteers who are willing to share their time, expertise and experience.

The Museum offers volunteer opportunities for teens and adults of all ages.

Benefits include:

  • Museum Membership
  • Backside Tours
  • Annual Horse Farm Tour
  • Personalized Outriders Jacket
  • All Outriders receive discounts in the Derby Caf√© and the Gift Shop
  • Community service hours or credits
  • Networking and experience in a potential career field
  • New friendships
  • Much more!
